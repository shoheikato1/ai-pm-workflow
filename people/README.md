# people/

Per-person or per-team context files. Loaded by Claude when that person or team comes up.

---

## What goes here

One file per person or team you interact with regularly. Useful for:

* Stakeholders whose context affects how you communicate with them
* Cross-functional partners whose team's goals you need to understand
* Anyone whose background, priorities, or working style Claude should know before drafting a message or meeting note involving them

---

## Suggested format

```markdown
---
name: [Name]
role: [Title, Team]
tags: [stakeholder, cross-team, leadership, etc.]
---

# [Name]

## Role and Focus
What they own, what they care about, what they're optimizing for.

## Working Style
How they communicate, what they respond well to, what to avoid.

## History
Key decisions, agreements, or tensions from past interactions.

## Standing Context
Anything that should always be in mind when working with them.
```
