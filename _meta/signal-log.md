# Signal Log

Append-only observations from each session. Captures how you think, decide, and communicate — the raw dataset for improving Claude's calibration over time.

Reviewed during weekly system review. Never edit or delete existing entries.

---

## What to capture

* **Implicit friction:** moments you corrected or redirected without explicitly calling it out
* **Decision patterns:** how you weighed tradeoffs, what you prioritized, what you deferred
* **Communication signals:** how you framed things for different audiences, tone shifts
* **Thinking patterns:** how you diagnose problems, what you notice first, how you connect dots
* **Voice calibration:** natural phrasing vs. what Claude produced, the gap between them

---

## Format

```
### YYYY-MM-DD — Session [ID]

**Friction signals:**
* [what happened, what it reveals]

**Decision patterns:**
* [tradeoff made, rationale observed]

**Communication signals:**
* [audience, framing choice, why it matters]

**Thinking patterns:**
* [pattern observed, context]
```
